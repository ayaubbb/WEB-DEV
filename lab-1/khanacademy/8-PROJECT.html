Travel to Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is about powerful families (Starks, Lannisters, Targaryens) fighting for the Iron Throne in the medieval-inspired land of Westeros, while a supernatural threat looms in the icy North; key themes involve politics, war, family loyalty, betrayal, and survival, all set in a world with long seasons and dragons, with memorable quotes like "Winter is coming" and "A Lannister always pays his debts".

The Traditional "Seven Kingdoms"

  1. The North: Ruled by House Stark.
  2. The Kingdom of the Rock: The Westerlands, ruled by House Lannister.
  3. The Kingdom of the Mountain and Vale: The Vale, ruled by House Arryn.
  4. The Kingdom of the Isles and Rivers: The Iron Islands and Riverlands, often united.
  5. The Kingdom of the Reach: Ruled by House Tyrell.
  6. The Kingdom of the Stormlands: Ruled by House Baratheon.
  7. The Principality of Dorne: Ruled by House Martell (initially independent).

The Nine Regions of the Realm (During the Show)

  • The North: Vast northern territory.
  • The Westerlands: Home to the Lannisters.
  • The Reach: Fertile south, ruled by the Tyrells.
  • The Stormlands: Southeastern coast, Baratheon lands.
  • The Vale: Mountainous northeast, Arryn lands.
  • The Iron Islands: Island chain, Greyjoy lands.
  • The Riverlands: Central plains, Tully lands.
  • Dorne: Southern desert, Martell lands.
  • The Crownlands: The lands directly around King's Landing, seat of the Iron Throne.
